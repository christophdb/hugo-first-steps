On our website, you have the opportunity to conclude and download your personal **Data Processing Agreement (DPA)** in accordance with Art. 28 of the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) with SeaTable GmbH.

{{< warning  headline="Note"  text="Currently, only a German version of the AV contract is available." />}}

## How to request a DPA

1. Open the page [Data Processing Agreement according to Art. 28, GDPR]({{< relref "pages/legal/avv" >}}).
2. Click on the link **to the form**.
3. A new page with an online form will open.
4. Enter your company name and address in the field **Controller incl. address**.
5. Select all **types of data** you wish to process with SeaTable.
6. Select the entire **category of data subjects**.
7. Enable the toggle **I agree to the agreement**.
8. Click **Create DPA**.
9. Your DPA will immediately open in a new browser tab.
