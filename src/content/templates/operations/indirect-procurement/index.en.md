Indirect purchasing usually accounts for between 15 and 30 percent of internal expenditure. At the same time, the procurement of indirect materials (such as office furniture, computers, pens, printer paper and coffee beans) is often neglected.

With this template, you can get started right away and set up your indirect purchasing efficiently and cost-effectively in SeaTable. You can define your product catalog centrally, define suppliers and provide your employees with a simple web form that you can use to enter orders centrally.

Read more about the possible applications of this template in ourblog article.
