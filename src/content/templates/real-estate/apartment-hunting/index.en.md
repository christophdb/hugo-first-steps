The search for the right apartment can quickly become chaotic - especially if you have several rental properties in your sights at the same time. With this template from SeaTable, you can record all the information on interesting rental properties in a structured and simple way in one place.

You can enter your individual criteria in the template and compare all potential apartments at a glance. In addition to pictures, you can also add the landlord's contact details and viewing dates. You can make a note of your impression after each viewing so that you don't forget any details. Good organization will make your apartment search and the final decision for your dream apartment easier.

You can find tips on how to find your new home and what you should consider before you start looking for an apartment in ourblog article.
