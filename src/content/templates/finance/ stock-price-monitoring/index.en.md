SeaTable is the perfect solution to continuously and automatically monitor the price development of stocks (or even prices).

You can leave the daily automatic data collection to the monitoring toolwachete.comand the automation servicezapier.com. With the help of free accounts with these services, you can document the share prices of any stock and keep a constant overview.

In addition to data acquisition, SeaTable offers you a wide range of options for displaying and evaluating the data in an appealing visual manner.
