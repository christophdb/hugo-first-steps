The biggest party of your life is coming up and you don't want to leave anything to chance? Keep an optimal overview of all participants, their wishes and the food and drinks they bring. With our party planner you simply keep an eye on everything.

Invite your friends via the web form and let them enter all the relevant information. When the web form is sent, the information is automatically transferred to the correct columns in your tables. You can use the statistics to create your own personal dashboard and see, for example, how many vegetarians will be at your party. You can also send a confirmation email to your party guests with just one click.

A helpful guide on how to plan a birthday party in just 3 steps can be foundhere. What are you waiting for? Your next party will be an unforgettable experience for everyone involved!
