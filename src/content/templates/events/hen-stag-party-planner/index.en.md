You have the honor of being the best man or maid of honor and now you need the perfect plan for the best bachelor party in the world? Don't panic, with SeaTable's template you can plan a stag party with ease!

Create an unforgettable stag party by creating a detailed daily schedule and using the map plugin to coordinate all the planned activities perfectly. Record all the important to-dos and distribute the tasks among the guests so that everyone knows what needs to be done. Invite guests using the web form and keep track of who has confirmed their attendance in both the list of participants and the gallery.

You can keep an eye on all your expenses in a separate table so that you don't go over budget. You also have the option of saving important documents such as order confirmations and receipts directly so that everything is in one place. So nothing stands in the way of the ultimate celebration!
