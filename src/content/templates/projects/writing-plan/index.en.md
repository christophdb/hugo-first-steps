Whether you're preparing to create a spooky crime novel, a captivating novel, or an informative nonfiction book, one thing is for sure: with this writing plan, you'll be well set for your potential bestseller. From summarizing your storylines to a comprehensive database of characters, locations, and objects, this writing plan has everything you need as a writer.

The plot outline allows you to not only capture the basic structure of your book, but also track the progress of each chapter. A table with the characters allows you to list all your characters and describe them in detail. From outward features to personality traits, you won't lose sight of important details.

The location map offers you the opportunity to describe all the locations of your book in detail. Create atmosphere and meaning behind each location. Use images to boost your inspiration and illustrate people, objects, or concepts.
